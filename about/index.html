About Us

Noran Transmission Pvt. Ltd. specializes in the manufacturing of high-quality lugs and ferrules, along with the supply of transformer components and pressboards. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we aim to serve the electrical and transformer industry with reliable and efficient solutions.

Who we are

Noran Transmission Pvt Ltd, founded in September 2023, is dedicated to delivering high-quality Copper Lugs, Aluminium Lugs, and Press Board components specifically designed for transformers. Our commitment to understanding and addressing the needs of our customers ensures reliability in every product we deliver.

At a glance

  • Location: Patiala, Punjab
  • Core products or services: Copper/ALuminium Lugs and Ferulles
